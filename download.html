    Subway Surfers

    About Game

    Subway Surfers is a classic endless runner game. The player controls a character who runs away from an inspector and his dog on railway tracks and through trains2. The game is similar to Temple Run and requires the player to dodge trains, trams, obstacles, and more to go as far as possible1. The game was first released in 2012 and has since been downloaded billions of times. The developer, SYBO Games, has been adding content ever since.
